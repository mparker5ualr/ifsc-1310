Assessment

The most important goal of effective communication is clarity. Clarity is not the same as simplicity.

Clarity means presenting information in a way that is easy to understand. Simplicity reduces complexity, but clarity focuses on making ideas understandable even when they are complex.

Good design helps users understand information quickly. A well-structured layout improves clarity without removing important details.

What is the difference between the "alt" and "title" attributes?

The alt attribute describes an image for accessibility and when the image cannot load. The title attribute provides extra information, usually shown as a tooltip.
